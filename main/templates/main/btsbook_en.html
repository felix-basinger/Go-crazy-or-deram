About the Author

Iryna Klypko is a 59-year-old Ukrainian woman, a mother of many children, a dreamer, and an inspirer who proves that life can always be changed if you follow your dreams. Her story is an example of how love for music and self-belief can become a driving force for transformation.

For a long time, her life was ordinary—family, work, routine. However, everything changed when she discovered the music of the iconic Korean band BTS. Their music became a source of inspiration and a push for change in her life.
